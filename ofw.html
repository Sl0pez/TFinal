The OFW Game

Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs) are vital to the Philippines' economy. They go abroad and look for a better life not for themselves but for their families. They sacrifice constantly to be able to send money home and ensure that their families are better off.

The OFW game is a short decision making game that is meant to emphasize show the hard decisions OFWs have to make almost on a daily basis. Their experiences obviously vary and they may not even make these same decisions. The game is not based on real experiences and the decisions come from statistics OFWs.

To play, click on the blinking text and then use ENTER, LEFT ARROW, and RIGHT ARROW keys to experience a small representation of an OFW's life.
