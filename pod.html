Three Generations of Food and Culture

Gabrielle, Elaine, and Melba are three generations of Filipinas who have spent considerable time in the Philippines as well as in New York. Melba is the grandmother who came to New York in the 1950s and started her family there. Elaine is her daughter who grew up in Long Island and then moved back to the Philippines to raise her family. Gabrielle is Elaine's daughter who grew up in the Philippine and moved to New York for university.

In this podcast, I acted as an interviewer, exploring how three different generations of Filipinas look at food and culture and how important it is for them.
