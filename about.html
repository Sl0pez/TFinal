about this project

Magasin is a transmedia project that tries to dispute the homogenous storytelling where all minorities, specificall Filipino-Americans, are made to look the same.

This project is not just for non-Filipinos. In fact, it was created with Filipinos in mind because of how culturally clique-ish we can be. Filipino-Americans are seen as other than those who have lived in the Philippines their whole lives. 2nd generation Filipinos also experience this discrimination that often goes two ways.

Magasin simply tries to redefine Filipino culture to encompass all Filipinos no matter where they live or how they live.

There are no pictures of the subjects anywhere on the website to prevent our natural instinct to make assumptions based on what people look like. Having no idea of what the people look like, we're forced to take the content in with an open mind.
